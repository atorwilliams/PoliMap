A note on detachment records

Detachment leadership turns over frequently enough that local records are often outdated or never updated in the first place. Most postings are short-term, and the RCMP does not reliably publish changes in command.

If you live in an RCMP detachment area and can find out the name of the current commanding officer, please get in touch so the records can be updated.
