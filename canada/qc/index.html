Heads up

Quebec is holding a provincial election in October 2026. The riding boundaries shown on the provincial layer are the new 2026 map, published in advance by Elections Quebec.

Because the ridings are being redrawn, some current MNAs won't appear in the correct riding until after the election. About 10 of 125 ridings are affected.

The federal layer is complete and up to date.

PoliMap is under active development. Data and features are being added regularly.
