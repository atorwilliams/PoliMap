Our Mission

Political representation should be transparent, accessible, and understandable. PoliMap exists to close the gap between citizens and their governments — one riding at a time.

We believe that knowing who represents you is the first step toward engaging with them. Whether you're looking up your MLA, your MP, or your local councillor, PoliMap puts that information on the map.
